How to Make Pizza

"

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, olives, peppers, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix warm water and yeast, then let it sit for 10 minutes until frothy.
  3. In another large bowl, combine flour, salt, and olive oil. Add the yeast mixture and knead into dough.
  4. Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  5. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  6. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top, and add your favorite toppings.
  7. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy!
